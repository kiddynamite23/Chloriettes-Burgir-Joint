How it all started..




The mindful creator



Chloriette's Burgir Joint started when a young youtuber, Jerico Lorenzo Misolas, thought of having a business

in order to invest his savings to the world of business. As you see from the image above, the Chloriette's Burgir Joint also started as a small place at the corner of the Malolos Street, in Makati. People seemed to like it that it sometimes causes a traffic due to how lots of people fall in line in patient, just to order our precious burgers. As a business man, he needed to have a bigger place in order to make place for not only people waiting in line, but for people who wants to eat on the resto also, And so he closed the food stall and sold the place in order to have a budget for a little bit more spacious area in another place.




The Small Expansion



As Mr. Misolas found a space for his little expansion, he started to share the small restaurant's new place so
